Videos: Function and Slide Check
  • First, ensure the weapon is unloaded and clear and insert an empty magazine.
  • Point the weapon in a safe direction utilizing a clearing barrel. When available.
  • Ensure the magazine is locked in the magazine well. Press the magazine. Catch and release the magazine.
  • Press up on the safety to ensure it's engaged and depress the trigger. Trigger should not fire.
  • Push the safety down to disengage the manual safety. Press and hold the trigger. The striker should fire and an audible click should be heard.
  • While holding the trigger. Pull the slide to the rear, the slide should lock due to the empty magazine.
  • Keeping the trigger depressed. Release the slide with the slide lock lever.
  • Release the trigger and audible clicks should be heard and felt.
  • First remove the magazine. Lock the slide to the rear and pull down on the takedown lever.
  • Next, pull the slide off the lower receiver and press down on the striker. It should not move.
  • Next, press down on the safety lock. The striker should fall.
  • This concludes your Weaponos Function Check and your Slide Check. Take care. Marines.
